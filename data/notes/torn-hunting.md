- Hunting (overview): unlocked at level 15, done in South Africa. Each hunt costs 10 energy and $500, with instant results (no wait). You gain hunting skill (starts at 1.0, max 100) and cash from the animals you bag. There are three session tiers - Beginner, Standard, and Advanced - that pay progressively more. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Hunting | 2026-07-17]
- Hunting - skill & income: reaching skill 100 takes roughly 164,000 energy (cut ~20% by a level-7 Meat Warehouse). Gains diminish fast - ~0.025/session at skill 40, ~0.0003 at skill 99. Move to Standard sessions around mid-20s skill and Advanced around mid-70s. At max skill a donator can pull ~800k per full energy bar. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Hunting | 2026-07-17]
- Hunting - boosters: income is boosted by donator status (more energy), Farm or Zoo employment (+25%), the faction Hunting perk (up to +30%), and Survival Skills education GEN2120 (+15%). World Tiger Day (late July) gives 5x hunting experience. Rare animals pay huge - e.g. White Tiger (~$5m, needs skill 22+), Cape Vulture (~$2.8m), Pangolin (~$1.4m). [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Hunting | 2026-07-17]
