- Medical items (overview): heal life and cut hospital time, but each adds to your medical cooldown (caps around 6 hours). Small First Aid Kit = +5% life, -20 min hospital. First Aid Kit = +10% life, -40 min. Morphine = +15% life, -70 min. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Medical | 2026-07-17]
- Blood Bags (medical): every type (A+, A-, AB+, AB-, B+, B-, O+, O-) restores 30% life and cuts 120 min of hospital, +30 min medical cooldown each - the strongest standard heal. An Empty Blood Bag drains 30% of your life to produce a bag matching your blood type. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Medical | 2026-07-17]
- Specialty medical items: Neumune Tablet cuts radiation hospital time by 3 hrs and radiation effect by 12 hrs. Ipecac Syrup actually ADDS 60-90 min of hospital time (used in the 'Dish It Out' OC, not for healing). Irradiated Blood Bag causes radiation poisoning (used in 'Clinical Precision' OC). [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Medical | 2026-07-17]
- Energy drinks (items): give energy on a shared 120-minute cooldown. By base energy: Goose Juice 5, Damp Valley 10, Crocozade 15, Munster / Santa Shooters 20, Red Cow / Rockstar Rudolph 25, Taurine Elite / X-MASS 30. The energy can be multiplied (faction +50%, books/events x2, up to x6 combined). [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Energy_Drink | 2026-07-17]
- Alcohol (items): each bottle gives nerve and adds 60 min to your booster cooldown; alcohol can push nerve over its normal max. By nerve: Beer/Champagne/Sake/Tequila = 1, Kandy Kane/Pumpkin Brew = 2, most Christmas/event bottles = 3-4, and the top ones (Moonshine, Green Stout, Christmas Spirit) = 5 nerve each. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Alcohol | 2026-07-17]
- Candy (items): gives happy and adds 30 min to your booster cooldown - people stack candy before gym sessions to boost gym gains. By happy: Lollipop / Bon Bons / Chocolate Bars / mints = 25, Chocolate Egg / Candy Kisses = 50, Bloody Eyeballs / Tootsie Rolls = 75, Reindeer Droppings / Truffles = 100, Sherbet / Humbugs / Pixie Sticks / Jawbreaker = 150, Birthday Cupcake = 250. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Candy | 2026-07-17]
- Booster cooldown (mechanic): separate from the drug cooldown. Alcohol adds 60 min to it, candy and energy drinks add 30 min each. Grocery Store (10%) and Restaurant (25%) company specials, plus the 'Self Control Is For Losers' book (-50% for 31 days), reduce consumable cooldowns. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Candy | 2026-07-17]
