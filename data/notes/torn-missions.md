- Missions (overview): Duke gives you randomly-generated contracts that use existing game mechanics (mostly combat). You get one new contract daily at midnight, holding up to 3 at once (5 with a 3+ star Logistics Management company). There are 5 difficulty tiers - Easy, Medium, Hard, Very Hard, Expert - and your standing rises as you complete them, unlocking harder, better-paying contracts. Declining a contract costs less standing than failing it. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Missions | 2026-07-17]
- Missions - credits & rewards: completing contracts earns mission credits, spent in the mission shop (books, weapon attachments, special ammo, weapons/armor). You can also earn credits by trading job points at a 10-star Private Security Firm. The shop shows 6 rewards at once and fully refreshes every 7 days. Expert contracts pay ~16x the money and ~5x the credits of Easy ones. For high-tier weapons/armor rewards, the game rolls 100 variants and gives you the best-quality one. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Missions | 2026-07-17]
- Missions - the agents: missions come from NPC "agents", each with a theme. George gives brand-new players their intro tutorial missions. Anonymous unlocks at level 5 and has one mission - an intro to Duke (complete 10 contracts) that grants an honor bar and 100 mission credits. Duke (the loan shark) is the main agent, with ~89 attacking-themed contracts. Professor Amanda Ravenscroft is a fourth agent announced but not yet released. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Mission | 2026-07-17]
- Missions - standing & difficulty: completing contracts raises your standing (which unlocks harder tiers); failing loses standing, and declining loses less than failing - so decline rather than fail if you can't do one. You always start an agent on Easy. Before each new tier you must complete a special unlock contract: Three-Peat unlocks Medium, Hobgoblin unlocks Hard, Cut Them Down to Size unlocks Very Hard, and Bring It! (defeat Duke) unlocks Expert. You can still be given lower-tier contracts after unlocking higher ones. A contract with an asterisk (*) is one you haven't had before. Finished contracts can sit unclaimed indefinitely until you click "complete". [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Mission | 2026-07-17]
- Missions - honors: three honor bars come from missions - completing the Introduction: Duke mission (the Protege bar), earning 10,000 mission credits, and completing 1,000 contracts. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Mission | 2026-07-17]
