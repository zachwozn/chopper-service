- Dirty Bomb (overview): a Dirty Bomb (DB) is a rare Special item BUILT by a faction (not bought from a shop) and used against another faction to cause massive respect loss. It's extremely rare (~41 in circulation) and carries a $50b networth valuation. There is no cash/credit "price" to buy one - the cost is in the faction requirements and materials to construct it. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Dirty_Bomb | 2026-07-17]
- Dirty Bomb - how to build it (the real "cost"): you must be a faction LEADER whose faction has all other armory upgrades, a 25-man capacity (20+ members), and you must have completed the General Science education course. Then buy the Laboratory upgrade for 105,027 respect and donate 100x Cesium-137 to the faction. Assembly takes ~200 hours (sometimes ~173-184), during which no member can leave or travel, and random members get hospitalized with radiation poisoning at the xx:01 tick. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Dirty_Bomb | 2026-07-17]
- Dirty Bomb - priming & planting: you must build it in a faction, but you do NOT need to be in a faction to plant one, and you can target any active faction (even your own) except admin test factions. A DB must be primed for 24 hours before use (this lets staff check it was obtained legitimately); trading it or putting it in a bazaar/display case resets priming for the new holder. Planting notifies everyone in Torn; the planter stays anonymous. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Dirty_Bomb | 2026-07-17]
- Dirty Bomb - detonation & respect damage: detonation happens 1-2 hours after planting. The target faction loses 10% of its respect PLUS a flat 100,000-150,000 respect (buffed in Dec 2025 from 5% + 70k-100k). If that drops the faction to 0 respect it is INSTANTLY destroyed and all its items, money, and points go to its leader - so any faction under ~110k respect is a guaranteed kill if hit. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Dirty_Bomb | 2026-07-17]
- Dirty Bomb - effect on target faction members: without a Hazmat Suit, members get radiation poisoning: 3-5 days hospitalized, battle stats cut by 99% for 21-30 days, and Energy/Happy/Nerve emptied. They can't leave or be kicked for 72 hours, all the faction's Organized Crimes are cancelled, and they can't take drugs while in radioactive quarantine. Territory fallout also wipes the faction's rackets and lingers on the city map for months. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Dirty_Bomb | 2026-07-17]
- Dirty Bomb - effect on random players & the city: online players not in the target faction can catch radiation from fallout (up to ~18 hours hospital) and have energy/nerve/happy zeroed; new players under protection are safe. The city enters a 4-hour state of emergency: airport closed (no new outbound travel), shops closed, subway and bus stations closed (blocking those Card Skimming/Search spots). Cesium can spawn on the city map afterward. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Dirty_Bomb | 2026-07-17]
- Dirty Bomb - protection & cure: the ONLY thing that prevents dirty bomb radiation is an equipped Hazmat Suit. Neumune Tablets reduce some radiation poisoning but you'll still wait a while. Normal medical items and revives do NOT work on radiation poisoning - only Hazmat (prevention), Neumune (partial cure), or natural decay. Related awards: Discovery (be in a faction that starts a build), plus RDD and Slow Bomb (both for using one). [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Dirty_Bomb | 2026-07-17]
