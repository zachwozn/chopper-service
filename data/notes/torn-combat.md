- Combat / Attacking (overview): attacking another player costs 25 energy. You get 25 turns (or 5 minutes) to win; running out of turns is a stalemate, running out of time counts as a loss for the attacker. Each turn has a ~1 second cooldown. You equip 3 weapons (primary, secondary, melee), 1 temporary item, and 5 armor pieces. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Attacking | 2026-07-17]
- Combat - who you can attack: you can't attack players who are actively traveling. You can attack players in the same location, except those "hiding out" (inactive 168+ hours) - those only appear as mission targets (highlighted pink). [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Attacking | 2026-07-17]
- Combat - attack outcomes (when you win): Leave = most experience, 15-30 min hospital for the target. Mug = steal 5-15% of their cash on hand, 30-45 min hospital. Hospitalize = 3-3.5 hours hospital but least experience. Arrest = requires a 3+ star Detective Agency. NPCs can also be Looted for special items. Losing puts YOU in hospital 15-30 min and breaks your kill streak. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Attacking | 2026-07-17]
- Combat - body parts & crits: 14 targetable body parts. The 3 critical spots (head, throat, heart) deal 3.5x damage; others range 0.7x (hands/feet) up to 2x (chest/stomach/groin). Base crit chance is ~12%, raised by education (+3%), merits (+5%), laser weapon mods (+5%), and weapon bonuses (7-21%). [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Attacking | 2026-07-17]
- Combat - escape, armor, group attacks: escaping is attempted after the defender misses - success is the attacker's dexterity vs the defender's speed. Armor reduces damage by a percentage: Damage = BaseDamage x (1 - Armor%/100). In group attacks, distraction stacks (up to 25 levels), halving the defender's Defense and Dexterity per level. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Attacking | 2026-07-17]
- Battle stats (the four): Strength increases the damage you deal per hit; Defense reduces damage you take per hit; Speed improves your hit accuracy and cuts opponents' escape chance; Dexterity improves your dodging, stealth, and escape while lowering opponents' stealth. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Battle_Stats | 2026-07-17]
- Battle stats - scale & training: trained mainly by spending energy at the gym; each stat grows exponentially. The old 50-million cap was removed in Aug 2022, so growth continues indefinitely (at a slowing rate). For scale: ~10m Speed = ~50% hit chance and ~640m Speed = 100%; ~10m Defense = 50% damage mitigation and ~140m Defense = 100%. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Battle_Stats | 2026-07-17]
- Battle stats - boosts: active boosts include gym training, booster items (+1% each), and education. Passive boosts include merits (up to +30% per stat), faction perks (+1% per upgrade), education, and company bonuses (25-50%). Drugs temporarily swing stats from -35% up to +50% depending on the drug. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Battle_Stats | 2026-07-17]
- Battle stats - hit chance by speed (rough, vs an opponent with ~10m dexterity): ~156k speed = 0% hit chance, 1m = ~11%, 2m = ~18%, 5m = ~33%, 10m = 50%, 20m = ~67%, 50m = ~82%, 100m = ~89%, 200m = ~94%, 500m = ~99%, and 640m = 100%. Speed also lowers your opponent's chance of escaping you. What matters is the ratio of your speed to their dexterity. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Battle_Stats | 2026-07-17]
- Battle stats - damage mitigation by defense (rough, vs an opponent with ~10m strength): ~312k defense = 0% mitigation, 1.25m = 20%, 2.5m = 30%, 5m = 40%, 10m = 50%, 50m = ~80%, 100m = ~94%, and 140m = 100%. It's the ratio of your defense to their strength that counts. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Battle_Stats | 2026-07-17]
- Battle stats - boosting gym gains: stack these before/while training - the book "Get Hard Or Go Home" (+20% to all gym gains, 31 days) or a stat-specific book (+30% to one stat's gains, 31 days); Sports Science education (+1% per relevant course); faction Steadfast perks (+1% per level in that stat's training branch); company gym specials (Fitness Center's Training Regime +3% all, a strip club's Boxercise +10% defense gym or Pilates +10% dexterity gym); and Sports Sneakers (+5% speed gains, bought in Dubai for ~$14b). Happy is still the single biggest factor in gym gains. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Battle_Stats | 2026-07-17]
- Battle stats - permanent stat enhancers: one-time-use items that add +1% permanently to a stat - Dumbbells (strength), Boxing Gloves (defense), Skateboard (speed), Parachute (dexterity); each costs 6 hours of booster cooldown to use. Certain books also give +5% to a stat (up to 10m) on completion (e.g. Brawn Over Brains for strength). [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Battle_Stats | 2026-07-17]
- Battle stats - passive boosts (hidden, apply in combat but don't change your shown number): merits give +3% per point up to +30% each (Brawn=strength, Protection=defense, Sharpness=speed, Evasion=dexterity); faction Aggression gives +1% speed & strength per upgrade and Suppression +1% defense & dexterity per upgrade; education adds passive totals (~+5% strength, +14% speed, +11% defense, +19% dexterity); and some companies give big passives - Gas Station +25% speed, Furniture Store +25% strength, Ladies Strip Club +25% defense, Clothing/Gents Strip Club +25% dexterity, and Lingerie Store +50% speed & dexterity when not wearing armor. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Battle_Stats | 2026-07-17]
- Battle stats - gear & radiation: some weapon bonuses raise stats mid-fight - Empower (strength while using that weapon), Quicken (speed), and Motivation (chance to +10% all stats). The Sentinel armor set boosts defense and the Vanguard set boosts dexterity. Radiation poisoning debuffs ALL your battle stats by a percentage based on how much radiation time you have left. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Battle_Stats | 2026-07-17]
- Battle stats - overdose penalties: overdosing on the drug Speed gives a PERMANENT -6 x your current level to Strength and Defense, and overdosing on PCP gives a permanent -10 x your level to Speed - so never OD on Speed or PCP. (The old 50-million "stat cap" that slowed gains was removed in August 2022; stats now keep growing exponentially, just at a slowing rate.) [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Battle_Stats | 2026-07-17]
