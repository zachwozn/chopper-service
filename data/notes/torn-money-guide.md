- Making money - new player path (Baldr's guide): money is hard until you hit level 15 and unlock travel - that's why rushing to L15 matters. After L15, running flowers or plushies nets roughly $2-3M a day (more if very active): fly abroad, buy flowers/plushies, bring them back to Torn, and sell for profit. A big chunk of Torn's economy runs on flowers/plushies because History-degree players trade full sets of either for 10 points (which sell for cash), so there's constant demand. [source: Baldr's Making Money guide (Torn forums) | 2026-07-17]
- Making money - Private Island for running (Baldr's guide): for flower/plushie running, live in a Private Island (PI) with a landing strip and a hired pilot. That gives 30% faster travel, +10 item capacity per trip (5 -> 15), no travel fees, and high happy for gym gains - so the PI pays for itself in extra profit. The landing strip is the one must-have upgrade; the best-value rentals are often around 3600 happy, but some with a strip go as low as ~2550 happy. [source: Baldr's Making Money guide (Torn forums) | 2026-07-17]
- Making money - trading (Baldr's guide): trading consumables makes the most money because people rebuy them constantly - drugs, alcohol, energy cans, donator packs, and flowers/plushies. Guns are poor to trade (people don't rebuy them). Trading needs high activity, a bazaar, and upfront cash; watch the item market and trade chat to buy, mark up, and resell. Big or bulk trades attract muggers, so be careful carrying cash. [source: Baldr's Making Money guide (Torn forums) | 2026-07-17]
- Making money - before level 15 (Baldr's guide): you can buy up to 100 items a day from Torn's own shops and flip them. Good picks: beer and basic plushies (kittens, sheep, teddy bears) from Bits-N-Bobs, or lollipops from Sally's Sweet Shop. Buying 100 and reselling makes roughly $50k-$70k profit a day - small, but one of the easiest pre-L15 incomes. [source: Baldr's Making Money guide (Torn forums) | 2026-07-17]
- Making money - jobs (Baldr's guide): always be employed, because work stats grow slowly and only while working. For new players the Sweet Shop job is great - low stat requirements, and at 7 stars or higher you can trade job points for energy cans that sell for $1.5M-$2.5M each. Basic city jobs (casino, grocery, army) also keep work stats ticking up. [source: Baldr's Making Money guide (Torn forums) | 2026-07-17]
- Making money - slutting / selling losses (Baldr's guide): the easiest good money when brand new. You attack someone and deliberately lose, which boosts their "defends won" count (worth merits to them), and they pay you about $250k-$300k per attack (don't sell for less than 250k). The xanax loop: ~4 losses earn ~$1M, buy a Xanax (+250 energy) and do ~10 more losses for ~$2.5M total from 100 energy - repeatable 2-3x a day. [source: Baldr's Making Money guide (Torn forums) | 2026-07-17]
- Making money - slutting cautions (Baldr's guide): it burns energy you need for gym training, so only do it short-term toward a specific goal (like affording your first PI rental), then stop. Find buyers in the defends sub-forum, read their post rules exactly (some require a heads-up message or fists-only, or they won't pay), and vet them - skip targets under ~$1B networth or under 100 days old, or with tons of enemies (possible scammers). Expect to LOSE; runaways pay more but are nearly impossible against much stronger players. [source: Baldr's Making Money guide (Torn forums) | 2026-07-17]
- Making money - mugging & jumpstart (Baldr's guide): mugging sounds lucrative but in practice most muggers waste energy and rarely find rich targets, especially new with low stats - avoid it until you're experienced and trained. To jumpstart: a donator pack is ~$5 for a month of donator status; some new players buy 2+, use one, and sell the others for ~$23M-$24M as starting capital. Giveaways and Discord/forum lotteries (Fun and Games subforum) are minor extra income. [source: Baldr's Making Money guide (Torn forums) | 2026-07-17]
