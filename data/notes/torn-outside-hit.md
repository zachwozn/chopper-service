- Outside Hit (overview): an "outside hit" is an attack that lets the hospitalized target use Early Discharge (leave hospital instantly with no medical items or cooldown used, health restored to what it was before the attack). It was introduced with the Medical Revamp 2021 to balance faction warring. An attack is an outside hit when the TARGET is on a territory-war wall or in a live ranked war, AND the attacker's faction is NOT in a territory war or ranked war with the target's faction. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Hospital | Outside Hit flowchart | 2026-07-18]
- Outside Hit - the two caveats: (1) Retaliation hits can NEVER be outside hits - a retaliation is a hospitalization within 5 minutes of the target attacking one of your members. (2) Attacks from Recruits (a faction member for less than 3 days) ARE outside hits, even if the two factions are at war. So if there IS a war between the factions and the attacker is not a recruit, it's an inside hit; if there's no war (and it's not a retaliation), or the attacker is a recruit, it's an outside hit. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Hospital | Outside Hit flowchart | 2026-07-18]
- Outside Hit - Early Discharge details: Early Discharge is available to non-recruits only (recruits were excluded as of 11 Nov 2025) and is immediately available at the very start of Ranked Wars and Territory Wars for all incoming outside hits. An alternative hospital icon appears when Early Discharge is available. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Hospital | 2026-07-18]
- Outside Hit - Elimination exception: during the Elimination competition (runs in September), ALL Elimination hits are treated as "inside hits", so they do NOT grant the target Early Discharge. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Hospital | 2026-07-18]
