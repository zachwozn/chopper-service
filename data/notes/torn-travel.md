- Traveling 2.0 (overview): Torn's travel system is being overhauled in phases (no longer billed as a fixed six-phase process - phases may be merged, added, or dropped). Phase 2 went live 23 June 2026 and introduced the Travel Inventory, doubled base luggage, cut all flight times, added foreign selling, and TEMPORARILY banned taking weapons/armor on flights abroad. The weapon/armor ban lifts during Phase 3 (staff say within a few months, before Halloween 2026). [source: https://www.torn.com/newspaper.php (Plane and Simple / Flight or Fight, Torn City Times) | 2026-07-17]
- Travel basics (wiki): you must be level 15 to use the Travel Agency in the City Center. Each destination has three shops - General Store (souvenirs, flowers, plushies), Arms Dealer (weapons and armor), and Black Market (drugs and contraband). Shop stocks are limited and restock on regular or irregular timers. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Travel | 2026-07-17]
- Travel Inventory (feature): a panel usable at all stages of a trip, even mid-air. It starts empty on the way out because nothing can be taken from Torn during Phase 2; you can use foreign items the moment you buy them (or on the flight home). You can also trash items abroad, and trash them mid-flight only in a light aircraft flying from an Airstrip. Overdosing mid-flight starts your hospital timer and can dump you in hospital on landing. [source: https://www.torn.com/newspaper.php (Plane and Simple, Torn City Times) | 2026-07-17]
- Inventory reconciliation: the wiki line "inventory access is unavailable while traveling" refers to your MAIN Torn item inventory, which is still locked while in flight. The new Travel Inventory (added in Phase 2) is the exception - it IS usable at all stages including mid-air, but only for items bought abroad. Don't confuse the two. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Travel + newspaper | 2026-07-17]
- Extra baggage / how many items you can buy or carry abroad (Traveling 2.0): base travel capacity was DOUBLED from 5 to 10 items for everyone. To balance that, suitcase bonuses were each cut by one - Small Suitcase now +1 (was +2), Medium +2 (was +3), Large +3 (was +4). Stock and other travel bonuses are unchanged. Max possible travel capacity is now 43 items, doubling to 86 on World Tourism Day (September 27). [source: https://www.torn.com/newspaper.php (Plane and Simple) + https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Travel | 2026-07-17]
- Travel capacity boosters (wiki): beyond the base 10, capacity comes from Airstrip/WLT/Business Class (raise the base toward ~15), suitcases (Small +1, Medium +2, Large +3), a 3-star Lingerie Store (+2), Cruise Line Agency (+2 at 3-star, +3 at 10-star), the Faction Excursion special (+1 per upgrade, up to +10), the book "Smuggling For Beginners" (+10 for 31 days), and a 7-star Flower/Toy Shop (+5 for those specific goods). [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Travel | 2026-07-17]
- Faster flights (Traveling 2.0): ALL travel times were reduced by 5.26% in Phase 2 (normal time fluctuations of about +/-3% still apply). Example: the standard Johannesburg/South Africa flight dropped ~16 minutes from 4h57m to roughly 4h41m. NOTE: older wiki time tables may still list the pre-cut figures (e.g. 4h57m to South Africa, ~26 min to Mexico) - subtract ~5.26% for the current times. [source: https://www.torn.com/newspaper.php (Plane and Simple) + https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Travel | 2026-07-17]
- Flight time reducers (wiki): standard times run from ~26 min (Mexico) up to ~4h57m (South Africa) before bonuses. Airstrip upgrade cuts ~30%, the WLT benefit ~27%, and a Business Class ticket ~70% (the fastest). The book "Mailing Yourself Abroad" gives another 25% off for 31 days. Business Class, WLT, Detective Agency special, Logistics Management special, and the 15-second attacking grace period were all left unchanged by Phase 2. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Travel + newspaper | 2026-07-17]
- Selling abroad (Traveling 2.0): you can now sell items from your travel inventory directly to foreign shops, but only items that shop already sells (more Torn items saleable abroad later). Sold items go into that shop's stock for other travellers. Buyback is about 75% of purchase cost - so you effectively pay ~25% to 'rent' weapons/armor abroad. Exception: the Black Market buys and sells at the same price. Wiki notes a 100-item sell-back limit per trip. [source: https://www.torn.com/newspaper.php (Plane and Simple) + https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Travel | 2026-07-17]
- Travel combat/safety (wiki): while you're actively traveling (status "Traveling to" / "Returning from") you CANNOT be attacked. Players in the same foreign country can attack each other, unless the target has been inactive 168+ hours ("Hiding out"). Newly arrived players get a 15-second grace period. A 10-star Theater residence hides your travel status and location from others. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Travel | 2026-07-17]
- Travel loadout (Phase 2 rule): when you fly abroad your weapon and armor loadout does NOT come with you - you land completely bare, and your original loadout is restored the moment you're back in Torn City (true even if you flew out with a full loadout before the ban started). Exception: the World Tiger Day event (end of July) is unaffected - tour guides supply hunting rifles and ammo for poaching. [source: https://www.torn.com/newspaper.php (Flight or Fight, Torn City Times) | 2026-07-17]
- Fighting abroad (Phase 2): when you land you can BUY and equip items on the spot. Only melee weapons, temporary weapons, and armor work abroad. Ammo-based weapons can be bought and equipped but won't fire (no ammo sold outside Torn). Clothing can also be equipped while traveling; if equipping clothing/armor bumps another item off, that item goes to your travel inventory - but only if you have a free slot, otherwise you get an error. [source: https://www.torn.com/newspaper.php (Flight or Fight, Torn City Times) | 2026-07-17]
- Travel loadout gotcha (Phase 2): equipping a new item abroad changes your loadout back home too. Swap your DBK for a Spear bought in South Africa and that Spear is still your loadout when you return. No items can be carried OUT of Torn regardless of where bought, so during Phase 2 you buy fresh weapons/armor each trip (and can resell them in the same country before leaving). [source: https://www.torn.com/newspaper.php (Flight or Fight, Torn City Times) | 2026-07-17]
- Drugs & drink abroad (Phase 2): drugs are the main useful non-combat item abroad. Xanax can be consumed right after purchase in the UK, South Africa, and Japan. Opium (South Africa, Japan, China) helps you break out of a foreign hospital - but only once travel-inventory access is added to the hospital page. Alcohol (Mexico, Japan) can be drunk but there's little point since there are no foreign crimes. [source: https://www.torn.com/newspaper.php (Flight or Fight, Torn City Times) | 2026-07-17]
