- Weapon bonuses (overview): weapons can roll a special bonus in one of three rarity classes - Yellow/Superior (most common), Orange/Epic (rarer), and Red/Legendary (rarest); a higher class gives a stronger version of the same bonus. Weapons with no bonus are called grey, white, vanilla, or non-legendary. There are ~67 bonuses total (14 unique to a specific weapon, plus 53 general ones). [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Weapon_Bonus | 2026-07-17]
- Weapon bonus mechanics: damage-over-time (DOT) effects (Burning, Bleeding, Poisoned, Lacerated, Severe Burning, Contaminated) last 5 minutes (a full fight) and are non-stackable with each other. Finishing-hit bonuses only proc if the weapon itself dealt the final damage (not a DOT tick). Bonuses that need a hit won't proc on a zero-damage hit. Rage and Fury can't proc together, and stronger non-stackable bonuses override weaker ones. Execute does not work on NPCs. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Weapon_Bonus | 2026-07-17]
