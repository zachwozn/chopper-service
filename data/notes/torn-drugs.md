- Cannabis (drug): +20% Strength, +25% Defense, +35% Speed, and +8-12 max nerve. 60-90 min cooldown, only 1 addiction point - the cheap, low-risk all-rounder drug. Overdose wipes your energy/nerve and hospitalizes you ~300-350 min. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Drugs | 2026-07-17]
- Ecstasy (drug): doubles your current happiness. 200-230 min cooldown, 20 addiction points. Overdose loses all your energy and happy. Best used right before a big gym session. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Drugs | 2026-07-17]
- Ketamine (drug): +50% Defense but -20% Strength and -20% Speed. 45-60 min cooldown, 8 addiction. Nasty overdose: ~1,000 min hospital and a 24-27 hr cooldown. Niche defensive drug. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Drugs | 2026-07-17]
- LSD (drug): +30% Strength, +50% Defense, +50 energy, +200-500 happy, +5 nerve, but -30% Speed and Dexterity. 400-450 min cooldown, 20 addiction. Long cooldown, big mixed effects. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Drugs | 2026-07-17]
- Opium (drug): +30% Defense, removes all your hospital time, and restores your life to 50%. 120-180 min cooldown, 10 addiction, and it CANNOT overdose. Great for getting out of the hospital. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Drugs | 2026-07-17]
- PCP (drug): +20% Strength and Dexterity, +250 happy. 260-400 min cooldown, 26 addiction. Overdose is brutal - permanent Speed loss scaling with your level, plus ~750-1,000 min hospital. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Drugs | 2026-07-17]
- Shrooms (drug): +500 happy but -20% to all battle stats and -25 energy. 182-237 min cooldown, 6 addiction. A happy boost that tanks your stats - time it away from fights. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Drugs | 2026-07-17]
- Speed (drug): +20% Speed, +50 happy, but -20% Dexterity. 250-352 min cooldown, 14 addiction. Overdose permanently cuts Strength and Defense (scales with level), so don't OD. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Drugs | 2026-07-17]
- Vicodin (drug): +25% to ALL battle stats and +75 happy. 240-360 min cooldown, 13 addiction, and the mildest overdose of any drug (just -150 happy). One of the best all-round stat drugs. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Drugs | 2026-07-17]
- Xanax (drug): +250 energy and +75 happy, but -35% to all battle stats. 360-480 min cooldown, 35 addiction. Overdosing means ~5,000 minutes in the hospital, so don't. It's the go-to energy source for training and crimes. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Drugs | 2026-07-17]
- Love Juice (drug): +50% Speed, +25% Dexterity, and attacks/revives cost 10 less energy. Valentine's Day event only, 50 addiction points, cannot overdose. Rare and powerful. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Drugs | 2026-07-17]
- Drugs (general): every drug except Opium and Love Juice has a small overdose chance on each use (cannabis is about 1 in 2,500). Overdosing racks up huge addiction and hospital time. Heavy addiction lowers your battle stats until it wears off. All drugs share one cooldown - you can't take another while one's active. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Drugs | 2026-07-17]
