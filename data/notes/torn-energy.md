- Energy (overview): energy is a finite resource shown by the green bar in your sidebar, used for most activities. It refills to maximum every 5 hours - donators get 5 energy every 10 minutes up to 150, non-donators 5 every 15 minutes up to 100. The absolute maximum energy you can hold at any moment is 1,000. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Energy | 2026-07-18]
- Energy from drugs: Xanax gives 250 energy and LSD gives 50 energy (as long as you don't overdose). Xanax is the main bulk energy source for training. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Energy | 2026-07-18]
- Energy from boosters: Energy Drink cans give 5-30 energy base (x1.5 if faction-made) and add 2 hours to your booster cooldown - e.g. Goose Juice (5), Damp Valley (10), Crocozade (15), Munster/Santa Shooters (20), Red Cow/Rockstar Rudolph (25), Taurine Elite/X-MASS (30). A Feathery Hotel Coupon refills 150 energy (donator) and adds 6 hours booster cooldown. A Green Easter Egg gives 500 energy, adds 6 hours cooldown, and can't be traded or sold. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Energy | 2026-07-18]
- Energy from company specials: many company specials trade Job Points for energy, capped at 100 JP/day on energy specials (resets 00:00 TCT). Examples: 1-star Furniture Store "Coffee Break", 1-star Pub "Pub Lunch", 1-star Restaurant "Free Meals" all give +3 energy per JP; 7-star Candle Shop "Reinvigorating Therapy" +5/JP; 10-star Game Shop "Overpowered" +5 energy +1 nerve +50 happy per JP; 10-star Farm "Early Riser" +7/JP; 10-star Television Network "Press Pass" has a chance to grant 300 energy for 25 JP and is NOT bound by the 100 JP/day limit. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Energy | 2026-07-18]
- Energy - other sources: a Point refill fills your bar to max once per day. Mc Smoogle Corp (MSC) stock gives 100 energy per stock block owned (up to 1,000), collectible every 7 days. Inactivity refills are free refills earned per week away (max 100 held). You can use at most 100 special energy refills per week. Each quarterly newsletter email has a one-time link worth 250 energy. During the Anniversary event, clicking the "T" of the TORN logo gives 50 energy (15-min cooldown, 10 uses total). Trick or Treat's Tier III "Dark Power" gives +5 energy per treat exchanged. Advanced weapons with the Revitalize bonus can proc energy (higher % procs more often). [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Energy | 2026-07-18]
- Energy - what it's spent on: gym training 5-50 energy per train (varies by gym); attacking a player 25; reviving 25-75 (depends on faction Fortitude/Reviver upgrades); searching the Dump 5; praying at Church 5; boosting Happiness on a games console 5; Hunting in South Africa 10 (plus $500) per session. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Energy | 2026-07-18]
- Energy - losing it: overdosing on certain drugs empties your whole energy bar; the drug Shrooms costs up to 25 energy; opening a parcel with a Horse's Head empties your bar; and radiation poisoning from a Dirty Bomb empties your bar. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Energy | 2026-07-18]
