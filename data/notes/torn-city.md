- City & areas (overview): Torn has a fully interactive city map split into districts. West Side: Education, Gym, Travel Agency. North Side: Auction House, Bazaar, Church, Item Market, Points Building, Points Market. Red-Light: Casino, Dump, Loan Shark, Missions, Raceway. Residential: Estate Agents, Properties. City Center: City Hall, Community Center, Hospital, Jail, Museum, Staff. Financial: Bank, Donator House, Stock Market. East Side: 14 shops (gun shop, jewelry, pharmacy, pawn shop, etc.). [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/City | 2026-07-17]
- City finds & shopping: items spawn on the city map daily at midnight TCT - you find them by zooming in and clicking, with higher Awareness improving finds. You can buy at most 100 items per day from East Side shops (resets daily; the Item Market and Bazaars have no such limit). City blocks can be claimed by factions as territory (base 15 respect, modified by sector, size, and density multipliers). [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/City | 2026-07-17]
- City - dirty bombs & map views: when a Dirty Bomb is activated in the city, several areas temporarily close - the Airport and all of the East Side except the Estate Agents and the Token Shop. The city is divided into 7 sectors, and the map has territory views for owner, racket, sector, density (green = lowest daily respect, red = highest), and size. Some city areas are only unlocked by leveling up or completing certain education courses. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/City | 2026-07-18]
