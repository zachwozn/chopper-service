- Ranked War (overview): a ranked war (RW) is a structured 1v1 faction-vs-faction fight you enlist for on the faction page's "Ranked" tab (need 10+ active members). Matchmaking runs weekly on Tuesdays at 12:00 TCT, pairing factions by rank and size. A war lasts a maximum of 123 hours and ends when one faction hits the target score or is destroyed. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Ranked_War | 2026-07-17]
- Ranked War - scoring: you score by attacking the enemy faction - each hit is worth roughly the respect it earns. Mugging only gives 75% of the score that a Leave or Hospitalize gives, so hit-and-leave/hosp scores more than mugging. Chain bonus hits add big score spikes, so chaining the enemy is key. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Ranked_War | 2026-07-17]
- Ranked War - target score to win: the net lead needed to win depends on both factions' rank and member counts. If nobody reaches the target within the first 24 hours, the target drops by 1% every hour after that, so wars always resolve before the 123-hour cap. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Ranked_War | 2026-07-17]
- Ranked War - ranks & divisions: there are 6 ranks - Metal (1 division), then Bronze, Silver, Gold, and Platinum (4 divisions each), and Diamond (4 divisions, requires 75+ members). Factions start unranked in Metal and climb divisions by winning wars. A hidden rank (based on the average stat-score of your top 80% of members) can override your visible rank during matchmaking to avoid mismatches. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Ranked_War | 2026-07-17]
- Ranked War - hospital & attack rules: during a war, a member gets 60 seconds of protection from outside hitters the moment they leave hospital or jail. Early Discharge is immediately available if you're being hit by outside attackers, so you can leave hospital without spending medical items. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Ranked_War | 2026-07-17]
- Ranked War - rewards: the winner gets a 2x reward multiplier, the loser 1x. Rewards scale with rank/division, your participation (0x up to 3x based on how much you contributed), member count (+1% per member beyond 10), and a random +/-10% variance. Points were removed as a reward; wins now pay out caches containing special weapons and armor. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Ranked_War | 2026-07-17]
