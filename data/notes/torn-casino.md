- Casino (overview): the casino runs on tokens - you get 75 free tokens per day at New Day. Most games cost 1 token per play. It's found in the Red-Light district of the City. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Casino | 2026-07-18]
- Casino - token sources: refill your tokens back to 75 by spending 30 points at the Points Building; earn 25 tokens per job point at a Casino City Job or a 1-star Cruise Line Agency (Bursar special); +50 tokens from a Casino Pass found in the City; up to 30 tokens from a successful Counterfeiting-casino-tokens crime (a failed one lands you in jail); a daily chance at extra tokens from the three wheels in Spin The Wheel (5, 10, or 25 tokens); and a random token reward from working a 10-star Television Network or from exchanging 25 job points for a "Press Pass". [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Casino | 2026-07-18]
- Casino - games: 11 current games - Poker, Bookie, Blackjack, Russian Roulette, Slots, Spin The Wheel, Roulette, Lottery, High-Low, Keno, and Craps. Chess (played against other players) was formerly available in the casino but is no longer a current game - the wiki lists it in past tense. Games unlock by level: Bookies (2), Lottery (3), Blackjack (4), Poker (5), Russian Roulette (6), Spin The Wheel (7). [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Casino | 2026-07-18]
- Casino - game notes: Blackjack uses 8 decks with Six Card Charlie. Slots' jackpot chance rises with bet size, up to a 64x multiplier at a $10m bet (2x at $100). High-Low limits buttons based on the dealer card (no Higher on an Ace, no Lower on a Two). Russian Roulette is a multiplayer game with a 3-minute timeout and can hospitalize you. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Casino | 2026-07-18]
- Casino - self-exclusion: you can ban yourself from the casino via in-game settings. A self-exclusion also blocks Lottery Vouchers, Casino Passes, Armor and Weapon Caches, and Cosmetic Caches. While banned you can't withdraw a cashier's check from a bookie bet until the ban is lifted, and you can't complete any accepted missions that require a trip to the casino. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Casino | 2026-07-18]
- Casino - awards: several honor bars are tied to casino games (a "Casino" honor set). [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Casino | 2026-07-18]
