- Casino (overview): the casino runs on tokens - you get 75 free tokens per day. Top up with points (25 points refills to 75 at the Points Building), earn 25 tokens per job point at a Casino City Job or Cruise Line company, +50 from a Casino Pass, or up to 30 from the Counterfeiting crime (with jail risk). Most games cost 1 token per play. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Casino | 2026-07-17]
- Casino - games: 12 main games plus PvP Chess - Poker, Bookie, Blackjack, Russian Roulette, Slots, Spin The Wheel, Roulette, Lottery, High-Low, Keno, Craps, and Rock Paper Scissors. Games unlock by level: Bookies (2), Lottery (3), Blackjack (4), Poker (5), Russian Roulette (6), Spin The Wheel (7). [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Casino | 2026-07-17]
- Casino - game notes: Blackjack uses 8 decks with Six Card Charlie. Slots' jackpot chance rises with bet size, up to a 64x multiplier at a $10m bet. High-Low limits buttons based on the dealer card (no Higher on an Ace, no Lower on a Two). Russian Roulette is a multiplayer game with a 3-minute timeout and can hospitalize you.
