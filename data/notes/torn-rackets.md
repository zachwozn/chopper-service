- Rackets (overview): a Racket is a criminal enterprise that spawns on random Territories on the city map, giving the faction that controls that territory a daily resource (on top of the territory's daily respect). Rackets spawn at Level 1 and occasionally level up or down between Level 1 and Level 5 (max); if a Level 1 racket levels down it despawns. Every extra level DOUBLES the resource output. Since a 2023 change, a racket produces nothing if the faction lacks the relevant armory. Across the map, total racket output is around $6 billion/day spread over roughly 160-180 rackets. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Racket | 2026-07-18]
- Racket types & daily output (Level 1 to Level 5): Illegal Casino - cash $25,000,000 to $400,000,000. Money Launderer - cash $10,000,000 to $160,000,000. Protection Racket - cash $5,000,000 to $80,000,000. Point Broker - Points, 500 to 8,000. Street Surgeon - Medical items 25-400 OR Pharmaceutical items 5-80. Bordello - Erotic DVDs, 5 to 80. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Racket | 2026-07-18]
- Racket types (continued): Arms Dealer - Temporary items, 25 to 400 (does NOT include pharma booster needles like Serotonin). Drugs Lab - Drugs, 25 to 400. Cannabis Factory - Cannabis, 25 to 400. Truck Stop - Energy Cans, 10 to 160. Bootleg Distillery - Alcohol (Moonshine), 10 to 160. The three cash rackets (Illegal Casino, Money Launderer, Protection Racket) are the most valuable.
