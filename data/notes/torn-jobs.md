- Jobs (overview): two kinds of jobs - City Jobs (starter jobs: Army, Grocer, Casino, Medical, Education, Law) and Company jobs (player-owned, see the company notes). City jobs need you to pass a 3-question interview about that sector; fail and you wait until the next day. You start a city job with 5 job points and are auto-fired after 400 days inactive. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Jobs | 2026-07-17]
- Jobs - city job perks: each city job grants job points and working stats (Manual, Intelligence, Endurance) daily, plus specials. Army = strength/defense boosts, weapon theft, stat spying. Grocer = cash/item theft. Casino = money and token bonuses. Medical = item theft and passive revive ability. Education = stat bonuses and faster course completion. Law = nerve/money/crime-XP gains and a jail-release ability. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Jobs | 2026-07-17]
