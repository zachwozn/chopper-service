- Blood bags & compatibility (overview): a Blood Bag restores hospital time and life (see the hospital note), but only if the donor blood type is compatible with the patient's (yours). Using an incompatible blood bag hospitalizes you instead. Standard blood-type compatibility rules apply. O- is the universal donor (works for everyone); AB+ is the universal recipient (can take any type). [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Hospital | blood compatibility chart | 2026-07-18]
- Blood compatibility - who can receive what: AB+ patient can receive from all 8 types (O-, O+, B-, B+, A-, A+, AB-, AB+). AB- can receive O-, B-, A-, AB-. A+ can receive O-, O+, A-, A+. A- can receive O-, A-. B+ can receive O-, O+, B-, B+. B- can receive O-, B-. O+ can receive O-, O+. O- can receive ONLY O-. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Hospital | blood compatibility chart | 2026-07-18]
- Blood bags - filling: fill an empty blood bag (bought from the Pharmacy for $15,000) after completing the Intravenous Therapy education. Filling takes 30% of your life, adds 1 hour to your medical cooldown, and gives one filled bag of your own blood type. Wrong/incompatible blood bags can be used any time (even at full life outside hospital) but will hurt you unless compatible. An Irradiated blood bag causes radiation poisoning.
