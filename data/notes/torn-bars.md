- Energy (bar): energy is your main activity resource, capped at 1,000 stored. It regenerates 5 every 10 min up to 150 for donators, or 5 every 15 min up to 100 for non-donators (a full bar takes ~5 hours). Spent on gym training (5-50), attacks (25), reviving (25-75), hunting (10), and small actions like dumping/praying/gaming (5). [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Energy | 2026-07-17]
- Energy - getting more: Xanax (+250), LSD (+50), energy drinks (5-30 base, x1.5 for faction members), company specials (job points for energy, capped 100 JP/day), MSC stock (100 per block up to 1,000), Feathery Hotel Coupons (full refill), and inactivity refills (up to 100/week). Overdosing, Shrooms (-25), and dirty bombs drain energy. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Energy | 2026-07-17]
- Nerve (bar): nerve is used for crimes (main use), busting others from jail (5 each), and busting yourself out (half your bar). It does NOT get used by faction Organized Crimes. It regenerates 1 every 5 minutes up to your max (hard system cap 32,767). [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Nerve | 2026-07-17]
- Nerve - max nerve: your base max grows with crime experience in +5 increments. Raise it further with merits (up to +10), the faction Criminality Nerve branch (+1 each, up to +40), and job specials like the Pub's Buzzed (+15). Get instant nerve from drugs (Cannabis 8-12, LSD 5), alcohol, a 30-point refill, or event items (Red Easter Egg +250). Overdosing or a dirty bomb empties your nerve. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Nerve | 2026-07-17]
- Happy (bar): happy mainly powers gym gains (the biggest factor at lower stats) and, in Crimes 2.0, boosts crime skill/exp gains. Base max is 100, rising with property up to 5,025 on a Private Island (temporary max can hit 99,999 for the Ecstatic honor). It regenerates 5 every 15 minutes (480/day) up to your base max, resetting on the :00/:15/:30/:45 ticks. Gym training burns 40-60% of the energy used as happy. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Happy | 2026-07-17]
- Happy - boosting it: candy gives 25-250 happy (tripled on World Diabetes Day, up to 9x with the book + faction bonus); Ecstasy doubles your current happy (200-231 min drug cooldown); Erotic DVDs give 2,500-5,000; Yellow Easter Eggs 10,000. People stack happy right before a big gym session for maximum gains. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Happy | 2026-07-17]
