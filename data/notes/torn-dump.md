- Dump (overview): the Dump is an area in the City (Red-Light district) that you can search for items. Anything players dispose of via "Trash this item" on their items page ends up in the dump and may be picked up by others (e.g. "You found the item Brick trashed by Chedburn!"). Warning: you CAN trash advanced weapons with bonuses, so be careful not to dump valuable gear by accident. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Dump | 2026-07-18]
- Dump - searching: each search costs 5 energy and has a chance to find a trashed item. Your odds of finding any specific item depend on how many different item types are currently in the dump - e.g. if there are 333 different item types in the dump and someone dumps a donator pack, you'd have a 1 in 334 chance of finding it. Items with lower total circulation have an added rarity factor. Awareness no longer affects dump searches (removed in an update). There's also a Duke mission where you must find an item in the dump and defeat its previous owner. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Dump | 2026-07-18]
- Dump - honors: three honor bars are tied to the dump - find 1,000 items in the dump, trash 5,000 items, and dump an item with a current market value of at least $1,000,000. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Dump | 2026-07-18]
