- Territories (overview): territories are parts of the Torn city map that factions can own for a small daily respect (plus any Racket proceeds). To assault or claim you need at least 10 members and room in your faction upgrades. A faction can control up to 17 territories (via upgrades). Your first territory must be claimed in the city outskirts (Sector 7); from there you can only claim/assault blocks adjacent to what you already hold. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Territory | 2026-07-18]
- Territory - respect value: each block has a base of 15 daily respect, multiplied by its sector, size, and density. Sector: x2.50 (Sector 1, Central) down through x2.25, x2.00, x1.75, x1.50, x1.25, to x1.00 (Sector 7, outskirts). Size: x1.00 (very small, smallest 5%), x1.25 (small), x1.50 (medium, middle 50%), x1.75 (large), x2.00 (very large, largest 5%). Density: x1.00 (empty fields), x2.00 (buildings), x3.00 (landmark). So central, large, landmark blocks are worth by far the most respect. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/City | https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Territory | 2026-07-18]
- Territory wars - the wall: when a territory is assaulted a single shared wall appears with 5-30 slots depending on territory size, auto-filled with the defending faction's strongest available defenders. You knock someone off the wall by forcing them out of "Okay" status (hospitalizing or jailing them), not just defeating them. Knock someone off while you're not on the wall and you take their spot; knock them off while you ARE on the wall and the spot is reserved for your faction for 60 seconds. Joining an empty slot costs 5 energy. Members who joined the faction after the war started can't join the wall. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Territory | 2026-07-18]
- Territory wars - scoring & duration: every second a member is on the wall gives the assaulting faction +1 point (defenders on the wall push the score back toward 0). The assault's goal is 50,000 x the number of wall slots (so between 250,000 and 1,500,000). If the goal isn't reached within 72 hours the assault fails. Wars last at most 3 days (72 hours), and the fastest possible win is 14 hours. War-related attacks (like removing someone from a war wall) give a x2 respect bonus. An assault can be ended early as a truce via a peace treaty - a trade between members of each faction who have territory permissions. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Territory | 2026-07-18]
- Territory - cooldowns: several 72-hour cooldowns apply. If you abandon or lose your last/only territory, you must wait 72 hours before claiming again in Sector 7. If you lose a war on a specific territory, you can't reassault THAT territory for 72 hours. And if you've warred a particular territory at all in the last 90 days, you can't war it within 72 hours of any other war on it - even wars not involving your faction. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Territory | 2026-07-18]
- Territory - the map & interface: on the city map, faction-held territories are shaded a set color and unclaimed ones are gray (a toggle below the map shows the full territory view). Clicking a territory shows its Name (ID), Sector, Size, Density, Slots (player slots for war), and Daily respect; if it's under assault it also shows war progress. Your faction's Territory tab shows a zoomed map of just your holdings plus related faction news. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Territory | 2026-07-18]
