- Territories (overview): territories are parts of the Torn city map that factions can own for daily respect plus racket proceeds. To assault or claim you need 10+ members and territory upgrade capacity (max 17 per faction). Your first territory must be in Sector 7, and later ones must be adjacent to what you already hold. Rackets on a territory start at Level 1 and can grow to Level 5 for more daily resources. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Territories | 2026-07-17]
- Territory wars: assaulting puts up a defensive wall (5-30 slots by territory size). Joining an empty slot costs 5 energy; score builds at 1 point/second per player on the wall. Victory needs 50,000 points x the number of wall slots. Wars last at most 72 hours, with a minimum 14 hours to win. Various 72-hour cooldowns apply after losing/abandoning a territory or warring a recently-warred one. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Territories | 2026-07-17]
