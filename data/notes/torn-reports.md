- Reports (rule reports): you report rule-breakers with the clipboard icon on a profile (you must be 24+ hours old to file one). Reportable things include multiple accounts / account sharing, hacking, spamming, profile/forum violations, bug abuse, discrimination, and cash-transfer exploitation. Retaliatory or false reports are forbidden and can get you jailed. Bugs go on the Bugs & Issues forum; offensive messages can be reported directly from the mail (even if anonymous).
