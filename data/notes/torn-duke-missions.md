- Duke missions (overview): Duke the loan shark gives daily contracts - simple randomly-generated tasks, almost all attacking-focused. There are ~89 different contracts. Below is each contract with its objective and a key tip. To find a specific one, look for its name. Unlock contracts gate the difficulty tiers: Three-Peat unlocks Medium, Hobgoblin unlocks Hard, Cut Them Down to Size unlocks Very Hard, and Bring It! (defeat Duke) unlocks Expert.
