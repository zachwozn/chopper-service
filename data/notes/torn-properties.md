- Properties (overview): your property gives base happy, which boosts gym gains. There are 14 standard properties from the free Shack (100 happy) up to the Private Island ($500m, 2,000 happy). Rough ladder: Trailer 110, Apartment 125, Semi-Detached 150, Detached 200, Beach House 300, Chalet 350, Villa 400, Penthouse 450, Mansion 500, Ranch 600, Palace 1,000, Castle 1,500, Private Island 2,000. Rare player-only properties go far higher (Queen Eleanor 7,975 happy). [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Properties | 2026-07-17]
- Properties - upkeep & rentals: daily upkeep runs from $0 (Shack) to $100,000 (Private Island); if unpaid fees hit 10% of the property's price your max happy drops toward 0. You can rent properties from landlords (paid upfront, terms locked once accepted, max 100-day lease, no vault access in a rental; leases end at 03:30 Torn time). [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Properties | 2026-07-17]
- Properties - upgrades & perks: Penthouse and up can add staff (maid, butler, guard, doctor, pilot; $5k-$150k/day). Key perk upgrades: Vault (Small $20m holding $100m, up to Extra Large $215m holding $1b), Medical Facility ($17m, +1% life regen per tick), Airstrip with Plane ($75m, 30% off travel times + free travel), swimming pool (+2% gym gains), Advanced Shooting Range ($250k, +2% damage), Private Yacht ($895m, 500 happy). ELT stock or a 10-star Property Broker give 10% off upgrades; Property Law education gives 5% off purchases. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Properties | 2026-07-17]
