- Properties (overview): your property's main purpose is to raise your max Happy, which boosts Battle Stat gains from the gym. Everyone starts with a free Shack (100 happy, can't be sold). There are 14 standard properties; the three most expensive (Palace, Castle, Private Island) also grant extra benefits beyond happy. Buy from the Estate Agents in the Residential district. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Properties | 2026-07-18]
- Properties - standard ladder (cost / base happy / daily upkeep): Shack $0 / 100 / $0; Trailer $5,000 / 110 / $10; Apartment $25,000 / 125 / $25; Semi-Detached House $75,000 / 150 / $70; Detached House $300,000 / 200 / $150; Beach House $500,000 / 300 / $200; Chalet $750,000 / 350 / $300; Villa $1,250,000 / 400 / $350; Penthouse $2,000,000 / 450 / $450; Mansion $3,000,000 / 500 / $500; Ranch $15,000,000 / 600 / $1,000; Palace $65,000,000 / 1,000 / $3,000; Castle $200,000,000 / 1,500 / $10,000; Private Island $500,000,000 / 2,000 / $100,000. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Properties | 2026-07-18]
- Properties - fully upgraded happy (with staff): a fully upgraded property gives much more happy than its base - Trailer 165, Apartment 188, Semi-Detached 275, Detached 500, Beach House 650, Chalet 725, Villa 800, Penthouse 1,150, Mansion 1,725, Ranch 1,925, Palace 2,550, Castle 3,475, and a fully-loaded Private Island up to 5,000-5,025 happy (with yacht and XL vault). A common Private Island sits around 4,500 happy. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Properties | 2026-07-18]
- Properties - facility upgrades (happy): Interior Modification (Sufficient = +1/4 of house happy, Superior = +1/2); Hot Tub +25; Sauna +25; Pool (Small +100 / Medium +125 / Large +150); Advanced Shooting Range +50; Open Bar +50. High-value upgrades: Vault (Small $20m holds $100m / Medium $42m / Large $98m / Extra Large $215m holds $1b) +50 to +125 happy; Medical Facility $17m (+50 happy); Airstrip with Plane $75m (+250 happy); Private Yacht $895m (+500 happy). Buying a lower vault tier doesn't discount the next. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Properties | 2026-07-18]
- Properties - staff (daily cost / happy): staff need a big enough property. Maid ($5k-$25k, +50 to +100), Butler ($7k-$22.5k, +75 to +125), and Guard ($15k-$75k, +100 to +300) need a Penthouse or bigger. Doctor ($5k-$150k for 1x-10x, +100 to +500) needs a Mansion or bigger. Pilot ($25k-$30k, +25 to +50) needs a Castle or Private Island. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Properties | 2026-07-18]
- Properties - the 4 extra perks (from upgrades): a Medical Facility gives +1% Life Regeneration per tick (needs a Doctor); an Airstrip with Plane gives access to fly, carries 15 items, 30% off travel times and free travel (needs a Pilot); a Swimming Pool gives +2% Gym Gains; and an Advanced Shooting Range gives +2% Damage. Only the Palace, Castle, and Private Island can take the vault/medical/airstrip high-value upgrades (yacht is Private Island only). [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Properties | 2026-07-18]
- Properties - discounts & selling: the ELT stock benefit and a 10-star Property Broker each give 10% off all home upgrade costs; LAW2910 education gives 5% off buying properties from the Estate Agents. Owned properties can be sold via the Selling Market or back to the Estate Agents (must be vacant), with any unpaid upkeep/staff fees deducted from the sale value. Estate-agent resale value is 75% of the purchase cost. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Properties | 2026-07-18]
- Properties - upkeep: both owning and leasing carry daily upkeep + staff fees (payable by you or your spouse). If unpaid fees exceed 10% of the property's un-upgraded cost, the max happy it provides gradually drops toward 0. If either spouse works at a 7-star Lingerie Store, the upkeep and staff costs for their residence are waived (existing debt isn't cancelled, it just stops growing). [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Properties | 2026-07-18]
- Properties - leasing: if you can't afford to buy, you can lease from another player. Terms (price, length, upgrades) lock once the tenant accepts and can't be changed by either side; the price is paid fully upfront (no daily fee to the landlord, but tenant staff fees still apply). You can't access the vault in a rented property. Max lease is 100 days; landlords can offer extensions/renewals during occupancy. All leases end at 03:30 Torn time regardless of start time; unpaid upkeep at lease end is transferred to Duke to manage. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Properties | 2026-07-18]
- Properties - moving & spouse: you must move into a property to use its facilities; to move out you first fire all staff and clear the upkeep balance (moving is done by moving into another property). Your spouse can move into any property you own/rent that isn't leased out (they can't hire staff), and you can share the property vault with your spouse (off by default, revocable). Moving to a new property leaves your old vault funds in place until you move back. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Properties | 2026-07-18]
- Properties - unique properties: beyond the 14 standard ones there are 12 unique properties, originally auctioned in 2011 for $12-23 billion each and now only buyable from their current owner (St Paul's Abbey sold for $70b in May 2020; the Royal Penthouse valued ~$75b in 2016). All have $250,000 daily upkeep. By max happy: Queen Eleanor 7,975, Drakkar Sea Fort 7,725, Eagle Island 7,275, Royal Penthouse / Cerium Temple / Trekant Tower / St. Pauls Abbey 7,225, Iron Fist Hill 7,150, Presidential Bunker / Maidengrave 6,775, USS Bloodbath 6,325, Silo X17 6,025. (Only some have airstrips or yachts; the USS Bloodbath is the only one without a pool, so it lacks the +2% gym gains.) [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Properties | 2026-07-18]
