- Lottos are my bread and butter. Start a standard one with /sl <prize>. Variants: /slq is a quiet lotto that auto-draws after 20 seconds with no pings, /sla is additive (entries stack into the pot), and /slf is faction-only. [source: FactionDiscord/bot/cogs/lottos.py (verified in code) | 2026-07-16]
- Running a lotto: people join with the button (or /join), and buy extra entries for 50 credits each with /buy. Draw the winner with /draw, or /cd to draw in 15 seconds. Check status with /chk, repeat the last lotto with /rl, and free up a stuck lotto lock with /unlock. Want it hands-off? /autocd auto-draws after a time or entry count (e.g. !autocd 10m or !autocd 5e). [source: FactionDiscord/bot/cogs/lottos.py (verified in code) | 2026-07-16]
- My minigames all take a prize and most accept an optional 'instant' flag: /fid (FirstID — first to type their Torn ID wins), /lid (LastID — last ID before time runs out wins), /mm (MathMatters — solve it first), /nti (NameTheItem), /aware (Awareness — 10 random members picked, first to click wins), /don (DoubleOrNothing — press your luck), /scratch (ScratchOff), and /ctw (ClickToWin). If a game gets stuck, a runner or admin ends it with /endgame. [source: FactionDiscord/bot/cogs/minigames.py (verified in code) | 2026-07-16]
- Banker: hit /banker <amount> to request money from a faction banker, or leave the amount off to check your balance. I take amounts like 'all', '1k', '1m', '1b' — I'm not picky. [source: FactionDiscord/bot/cogs/banker.py (verified in code) | 2026-07-16]
- Bookie tracking: paste the bet line from Torn into /bet, log the outcome with /result, and see your record (total net, wins, losses, prediction ratio) with /bookie. Yeah, I'll happily run /bookie on other people too, if you want to see how bad they are. [source: FactionDiscord/bot/cogs/bookie.py (verified in code) | 2026-07-16]
- Torn lookups: /profile (or /p) <id_or_name> (stats, level, faction, honors), /company (/comp), /stock <symbol> plus /destination (/dest) for overseas stock, /gym, /honor, /item (/ins), /hof (hall of fame), and /searchforcash (/sfc) for SFC crime info. Need to hit someone? /attack and /bounty hand you the links. [source: FactionDiscord/bot/cogs/torn.py (verified in code) | 2026-07-16]
- Player tools: /stats shows your battle stats with modifiers (and auto-deletes after 30 seconds so nobody screenshots you), /merits shows your merit allocations, /bust requests a bust, and /bestoc (or /boc) sets the highest OC you can do for spawning. Drop your Torn API key in with /apikey so my stats and auto-send stuff actually works. I also watch your bars and cooldowns — energy, nerve, drugs, boosters, hospital, travel — and ping you when they're ready. You're welcome.
