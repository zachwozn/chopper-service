- Torn staff (overview): Torn's official staff run the game and enforce the rules. The public roster lives at torn.com/staff.php. Roles from most to least senior on the game-management side are Admin, Officer, Moderator, and Helper; a large separate Developer team builds the game, with some tagged Quality Assurance, and a few Testers. This is a mid-2026 snapshot and the roster changes over time. [source: https://www.torn.com/staff.php | 2026-07-17]
- Staff roles (what they do): Admins run the whole operation. Officers handle player security and wellbeing, script moderation, staff training, social media and events. Moderators moderate user-generated content (forums, profiles, etc.). Helpers assist users and do public moderation. Developers build and maintain the game; QA/Testers test it. [source: https://www.torn.com/staff.php | 2026-07-17]
- Who created / made / founded Torn: Torn (aka Torn City) was created by Joe Chedburn - known in-game as Chedburn [player ID 1], or just "Ched". He is the game's founder and owner. He began building Torn in 2004 (at age 16, in PHP) and released it on 15 November 2004 under his company Chedburn Networks. So the answer to "who made Torn", "who created Torn", or "who is the founder of Torn" is: Chedburn (Joe Chedburn). [source: https://www.torn.com/staff.php | Torn history | 2026-07-18]
