- Energy is a core player resource in Torn City, displayed as an energy bar that regenerates passively over time. The base maximum energy is 100, increased to 150 for donators or subscribers. Energy is used for gym training to increase battle stats including Strength, Defense, Speed, and Dexterity. [source: https://grokipedia.com/page/Energy_Torn_City — VERIFIED against https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Energy | 2026-07-15]
- Optimal gym ratios in Torn City involve unbalanced distributions of training time across the four battle stats (Strength, Defense, Speed, Dexterity) to maximize efficiency through special gym bonuses. Hank's ratio is 35% Strength, 28% Defense, 28% Speed, and 9% Dexterity. [source: https://grokipedia.com/page/Optimal_Gym_Ratios_Torn_City — unverified | 2026-07-15]
- Energy regenerates at a base rate of 5 energy every 15 minutes for non-donator players, and 5 energy every 10 minutes for donators or subscribers. The maximum energy capacity is 100 for non-donators and 150 for donators or subscribers. - PRIMER (crimes 2.0 overview): Crimes 2.0 released 20 June 2023. It runs two parallel counters: Crime Experience (CE, hidden, affects ALL crimes' and OCs' success rates) and Crime Skill (CS, per-crime, 1-100). CS is self-contained per crime (skill 50 in one crime does nothing for another); only CE spans all crimes. Crimes are ordered on the page by complexity (left-right, top-bottom); more complex crimes (lower on the page) give more CE per nerve spent. [source: https://www.torn.com/forums.php?t=16385729 (Emforus, Crimes 2.0: A Criminal Primer, pasted by Zach) | 2026-07-16]
- PRIMER (crimes 2.0, NNB): Natural Nerve Bar is set by hidden Crime Experience, starting at 15 NNB for new 2.0 players. Unlike 1.0 (capped at 60), 2.0 NNB grows indefinitely, each increase needing exponentially more CE (highest known ~105). NNB increases are semi-random once you have enough CE, but decreases are instant if CE drops. Since CE is hidden, players use NNB as the proxy for judging what advanced crimes/OCs they can attempt. [source: https://www.torn.com/forums.php?t=16385729 (Emforus Primer) | 2026-07-16]
- PRIMER (crimes 2.0, skill curve): Crime Skill rises fast early and slows near 100 (roughly: CS50 = 25% of the way to max nerve-wise, CS80 = 50%, CS91 = 75%).
