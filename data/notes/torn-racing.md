- Racing / Raceway (overview): the Torn City Raceway is in the Red-Light district of the City (added 1 March 2007). You need a Car and a Racing License (bought at the Points Building for 50 points) to race. Cars come from The Docks, the Item Market/Bazaars, or the Grand Theft Auto crimes on the old Crimes 1.0 system (if you haven't migrated to Crimes 2.0). You can only be in one race at a time and can't travel while racing; being hospitalized mid-race doesn't affect the outcome. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Raceway | 2026-07-18]
- Racing - official races: free to enter, automatically pairing you with five other racers in the same racing class. An official race takes 1 hour to prepare and ends once all participants finish. After leaving an official race there's a 15-minute penalty before you can join another. Official races give DOUBLE racing skill, including gold-flaired events like the URT, Winter Rally, and Justice League Tournament. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Raceway | 2026-07-18]
- Racing - custom races: players create custom events (no racing points earned, but you still gain driver skill, and no wait period to join/create after leaving one). Setup options: race name, drivers required (max 6), max drivers (max 100), track, laps (max 100), cars/classes allowed, upgrades allowed or stock-only, bet amount (max $100m, winnings split among top 3), wait time before start (max 2880 min), optional password. A custom race with only one player times out and deletes 1 hour after the wait ends; if you leave or it times out after betting, the bet is refunded as a cashier's check at the bank. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Raceway | 2026-07-18]
- Racing - classes & points: you start in Class E and rank up by earning Racing Points from top-3 finishes in OFFICIAL races (1st = 3 points, 2nd = 2, 3rd = 1). Class thresholds: E = 0, D = 25, C = 100, B = 250, A = 475 racing points earned. Points still count toward ranking even if you later spend or lose them in a crash; points from Company specials do NOT count toward class rank - only points earned from racing. You can't enlist a car in a higher class than your rating but can use lower-class cars. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Raceway | 2026-07-18]
- Racing - racing skill: racing skill is gained every race (official or custom), capped at 100 (progression was overhauled 11 June 2025). Skill gain is affected by: total race length (longer races like 100 laps give more, but not strictly linear), number of players racing (more competition = more skill for everyone, up to 100 racers), your placement (higher finish = more skill), official races (double skill gain), and owning the Torn City Motors (TCM) stock benefit block (+10% racing skill). [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Raceway | 2026-07-18]
- Racing - tracks: there are 16 tracks, some gated behind reaching a certain class - Uptown (2.25mi), Withdrawal (3.4mi), Underdog (1.73mi), Parkland (3.43mi), Docks (3.81mi, the longest), Commerce (1.09mi), Two Islands (2.71mi), Industrial (1.35mi), Vector (1.16mi), Mudpit (1.06mi), Hammerhead (1.16mi), Sewage (1.5mi), Meltdown (1.2mi), Speedway (0.9mi, the shortest), Stone Park (2.08mi), and Convict (1.64mi). [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Raceway | 2026-07-18]
- Racing - cars & upgrades: you can name an enlisted car (up to 22 characters) via the pencil icon. Upgrades can't be removed once fitted - only replaced by buying a different upgrade for that slot (e.g. Adjustable vs Fixed Spoiler). Removing a car from enlistment strips all upgrades and returns the stock vehicle to your inventory; wrecking a car can also remove fitted upgrades. Finished races can be replayed from the race log at speeds up to x100. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Raceway | 2026-07-18]
- Racing - honors: six honor bars come from racing - reach Class A, 250 wins in the same car, reach driver skill 10, crash during a race, win 100 races, and earn 2,500 Racing Points. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Raceway | 2026-07-18]
- Racing - Ultimate Racing Tournament (URT): held twice a year to test racing skill and luck; the winner gets a Stormatti Casteon car and a Gold Racing Trophy. (See the URT note for details.) [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Raceway | 2026-07-18]
