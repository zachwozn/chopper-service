- Racing / Race Track (overview): the Race Track is in the Red-Light area and needs a Racing License (50 points from the Points Building) to enter. You race in classes E to A, ranking up by total racing points earned (E=0, D=25, C=100, B=250, A=475) - points still count toward ranking even if you spend or lose them. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Race_Track | 2026-07-17]
- Racing - points, skill & races: racing points come only from Official Races by finishing top 3 (1st=3, 2nd=2, 3rd=1); points from company specials don't count toward class rank. Official races are free, take 1 hour to prep, have a 15-min gap between races, and match you against similar car stats. Racing Skill is separate and earned in both Official and Custom races. There are 16 tracks with class-based access. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Race_Track | 2026-07-17]
