- Hall of Fame (overview): the Hall of Fame ranks every player across many stats, and you can see how many ranks you've gained this day/week/month. Rankings include Level, Offences, Rank, Attacks, Defends, Busts, Revives, Travel Time, Networth, Battle Stats, Work Stats, and Awards, plus faction ones (Faction Rank, Respect, Chains). Racing rankings (Wins, Points, Skill) live in the Race Track area instead. Some rankings hide names (e.g. battle stats) but still show your position.
