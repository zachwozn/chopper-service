- Marriage (overview): to marry you need a ring (Diamond, Gold, Sapphire, Plain Silver, or Cluster), plus a witness from your friends list, and both spouses and the witness present in Torn. You get a ring from the Item Market, the Jewelry Shop, or a 4-nerve Crimes 1.0 crime. Process: propose with the ring, they accept, both go to the Church, add witnesses, exchange vows and kiss. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Marriage | 2026-07-17]
- Marriage - benefits & divorce: spouses can live in each other's properties and optionally share vault access. Staying married earns anniversary honors and medals (250, 750, 1,500 days and beyond). You can't divorce until you've been married at least 7 days, and you must first remove your spouse from your property (or leave theirs). [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Marriage | 2026-07-17]
