- Loan Shark / Duke (overview): the city Loan Shark is Duke, found in the Red-Light district. You can borrow up to 20% of the money you've earned from crimes (your "old networth"), and that limit rises as you improve your credit rating with Duke by paying fees back on time. Duke charges 2.5% interest per week on your outstanding balance. The maximum loan is $2,500,000,000. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Loan_Shark | 2026-07-18]
- Loan Shark - fee cap: to stop fees getting exorbitant, Duke only grows your fees until they equal your current loan amount, then they stop. Example: if you owe $1,000, fees are capped at $1,000; once fees hit the loan value they stop increasing until they drop below that cap again. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Loan_Shark | 2026-07-18]
- Loan Shark - not paying back: after about 8 weeks of unpaid fees, Duke starts attacking and bountying you. His attacks are mugs, and he knows when you're carrying large sums of cash on hand. Anything Duke mugs from you is NOT deducted from your loan total (it doesn't pay the loan down). Duke is also one of the mission agents and may assign you contracts. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Loan_Shark | 2026-07-18]
- Loan Shark - Tim (old loan shark): Tim was the original city Loan Shark, removed on 11 Feb 2015 and replaced by Duke. Tim let you borrow up to $100,000 (upgradable by +$100,000 per 100 Points, repeatable) and charged 1% of the loan every day, taken automatically from your cash on hand as interest only - it never reduced the balance and could push your cash negative. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Loan_Shark | 2026-07-18]
