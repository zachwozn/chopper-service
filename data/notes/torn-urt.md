- URT / Ultimate Racing Tournament (overview): a semi-annual racing tournament held at the Race Track, running since 2017. It happens twice a year - a Summer season and a Winter season. The champion walks away with a huge prize package. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Ultimate_Racing_Tournament | 2026-07-17]
- URT prizes: the winner receives $1 billion, a top-tier trophy car, and a Gold Racing Trophy. (The wiki lists the car as a Bugatti Veyron; the in-game car prize for a given season can vary - e.g. the Summer 2026 edition offered a Stormatti Casteon.) [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Ultimate_Racing_Tournament + Torn City Times | 2026-07-17]
- URT qualifying: to reach the main tournament you must first get through a qualifying phase run across 4 different tracks. The 12 fastest times on each track advance. By entering the URT, all qualifiers agree to have their Driver Skill rating published publicly (it gets printed in the newspaper). [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Ultimate_Racing_Tournament | 2026-07-17]
- URT scale (recent edition): the Summer 2026 URT featured 64 of Torn's finest drivers competing for the $1 billion cash prize and the URT Summer crown, per the Torn City Times. [source: https://www.torn.com/newspaper.php (The Billion Dollar Race, Torn City Times) | 2026-07-17]
