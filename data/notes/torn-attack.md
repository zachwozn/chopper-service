- Attack (overview): an attack uses Energy to fight another player to put them in hospital, gain respect, or mug cash. It costs 25 energy per attack (only 15 while under Love Juice during the Valentine's event). Once started you have 25 "turns" or five minutes to win; a turn is using a weapon/temporary item, reloading, or attempting to escape, with a 1-second cooldown between attacking turns. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Attack | 2026-07-18]
- Attack - who you can hit: you can't attack a player who is actively traveling ("Traveling to/Returning from"). You can attack players in the same country as you, unless their last action is over 168 hours ago - then they're "Hiding out" and don't show on that country's People list. A hiding-out player CAN still be attacked if they're your mission target (they appear at the top of the People list, highlighted pink). There's a 15-second attack grace period right after you arrive from traveling. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Attack | 2026-07-18]
- Attack - body parts & damage: there are 14 body parts you can hit. Three are critical hits dealing the most damage - Head, Throat, and Heart, each a 3.5x damage multiplier. Chest, Stomach, and Groin are 2x. Left/right Arm and left/right Leg are 1x. Left/right Hand and left/right Foot are 0.7x (the weakest). (These multipliers come from large player data-sets, not official confirmation.) [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Attack | 2026-07-18]
- Attack - critical hit rate: base critical hit chance is 12%. It increases with: Education BIO2410 Anatomy (+3%), Critical Hit Rate merit (+0.5% per upgrade, up to +5% total), Laser weapon mods (+2% to +5%), a weapon with the Expose bonus (+7% to +21%), and Sylo Tooth (+25%). Separately, Throat damage can be raised +10% with Education BIO2380 Fundamentals of Neurobiology. The crit formula is simply flat percentages added together. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Attack | 2026-07-18]
- Attack - hit distribution (at base 12% crit rate): critical hits land ~80% on the Head, ~10% Throat, ~10% Heart. Of non-critical hits, the rough spread is Chest ~25%, Stomach ~20%, Legs ~20%, and ~10% each to Arms, Hands, and Feet, with Groin ~5%. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Attack (targeting diagram) | 2026-07-18]
- Attack - winning outcomes: Leave gives the attacker the MOST level experience and the defender the LEAST hospital time (15-30 min). Mug steals a percentage of the defender's cash-on-hand (roughly 5-15%; a 2019 update set the rate to a flat 5-10%, higher amounts rarer), gives reduced respect/XP vs leaving, and ~30-45 min hospital. Hospitalize puts the defender in hospital the longest (base 3-3.5 hours) but gives the least XP - used for retaliation, bounties, or missions. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Attack | 2026-07-18]
- Attack - other winning outcomes: Arrest sends a defeated player to jail for a cash reward (requires being in a 3+ star Detective Agency). Loot works on certain NPC accounts, giving attackers special NPC items via a lottery (number of winners depends on the NPC's Loot Level at defeat). Some missions require special post-defeat options like "Kiss", "Interrogate", or "Secrete <object>" - these give the same XP as leaving. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Attack | 2026-07-18]
- Attack - not winning: Escape can be attempted the turn after the defender misses; it succeeds if the defender misses again (based on attacker's dexterity vs defender's speed) - hitting for zero damage or using a non-damaging temp counts as a miss. Stalemate (25 turns, nobody wins) gives no respect and does NOT reset your kill streak. Losing sends you to hospital 15-30 min and breaks your kill streak. Timeout (5 minutes elapse) also sends you to hospital and breaks your streak - it counts as a loss for you and a "defend won" for the defender. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Attack | 2026-07-18]
- Attack - interruptions: some results don't count as a win/loss/stalemate/defend for anyone. "<Player> took down your opponent" (your target lost their own fight first); "<Player> defeated <Defender>" (in a group attack, only one lands the finishing blow - the others get an assist); "<Defender> was sent to hospital" (e.g. a Small Explosive parcel, Leslie from Spin The Wheel, or an OC result); and "<Defender> was surrounded by police" (since 21/09/21 only organised crimes cause this). [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Attack | 2026-07-18]
- Attack - equipment: you can equip three weapons (primary, secondary, melee), one temporary item (grenades or temp stat boosters), and five pieces of armor. Primary/secondary weapons are guns needing ammo, so you must reload (a turn) and can run out mid-fight. Melee weapons need no ammo but can't take weapon mods. Armor reduces damage by its rating as a percentage: damage taken = initial damage x (1 - armor/100), so a 40-rated piece cuts a 1000 hit to 600. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Attack | 2026-07-18]
- Attack - group attacks & distraction: when multiple players attack one defender, every extra attacker (beyond the first) who acts within the last 30 seconds adds a level of "distraction", capped at 25 levels. Aggression (Strength & Speed): distraction gives the defender only a "1 in number-of-attackers" chance to make a turn back (2 attackers = 50% chance each). Suppression (Defense & Dexterity): the defender's effective Defense and Dexterity are halved for each active distraction level. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Attack | 2026-07-18]
- Attack - settings: in Preferences you set a stealth preference ("Whenever possible" or "Off") and whether to re-equip temps after use (faction-loaned temps re-equip first). Defending uses four ratio sliders (primary/secondary/melee/temporary, 0-100) that set how often each is used - e.g. 100/75/50/25 (total 250) means a 100/250 chance of primary that turn, etc.; an empty/out-of-ammo slot counts as 0. Reloads can be toggled on/off per gun. If unlocked, you choose Fists or Kick when fighting unarmed (you fight unarmed if no weapons are equipped or all settings are effectively 0). [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Attack | 2026-07-18]
- Attack - life & regen (Attacking 2.0): max life ranges from 100 to 5,000 based on level, up to 7,500 at level 100 with merits. Life regenerates 5% every 5 minutes at base (full in ~100 min); a medical facility with doctors raises this to 6% every 5 min (~85 min). [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Attack | 2026-07-18]
- Attack - related awards: attacking honors/awards include Kill Streaker (10 kill streak), Chainer (participate in a 10-length chain), Carnage (a single hit earning your faction 10+ respect), Leonidas (a finishing hit with Kick), and Flatline (a one-hit kill). There are also attacking medals like "attacks won" and "respect earned". [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Attack | 2026-07-18]
