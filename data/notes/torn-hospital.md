- Hospital (overview): you get hospitalized by losing fights, failing some crimes/OCs, losing Russian Roulette, bad casino outcomes, training at the gym with drug addiction over 9%, incompatible/irradiated blood bags or Ipecac Syrup, faction targeting, or dirty bombs. Base hospital time is 180-210 minutes and can be extended. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Hospital | 2026-07-17]
- Hospital - getting out via revive: being revived removes you from hospital and restores a % of life based on the reviver's revive skill (0-100). It costs the reviver 75 energy (cut to 25 via faction Reviving perk, or 15 during the Valentine's event). [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Hospital | 2026-07-17]
- Hospital - medical items: Small First Aid Kit (-20-30 min, +5-7.5% life), First Aid Kit (-40-60 min, +10-15%), Morphine (-70-105 min, +15-22.5%), Blood Bags (-120-180 min, +30-45%). Effectiveness rises up to +50% via education (+20%) and faction upgrades (+30%). Each item adds to your medical cooldown; once it passes 6 hours (max 9 with faction upgrades) no more medical items work until it drops back. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Hospital | 2026-07-17]
- Hospital - Opium, Neumune & early discharge: Opium removes ALL standard hospital time, restores 50% life, and gives +30% passive defense - it uses the drug cooldown (120-180 min) instead of medical cooldown. Neumune Tablet is the only item that removes hospital time when you have radiation poisoning. During Ranked/Territory Wars, non-recruits can use Early Discharge to leave with no items or cooldown used. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Hospital | 2026-07-17]
- Hospital - what you can/can't do: while hospitalized you CAN use medical items, drugs, and consumables, open parcels/supply packs, and manage trades. You CANNOT use boosters/eggs/stat enhancers, deposit to the faction vault, or (if radiation-poisoned) use drugs. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Hospital | 2026-07-17]
