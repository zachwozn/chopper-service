- Factions (overview): a faction is a group of players under one name and banner, led by a Leader and optional Co-leader. You can apply to up to 10 at once. After being accepted you're a Recruit for 72 hours (limited permissions) before moving to the faction's default position. Factions share an armory and run organized crimes together. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Faction | 2026-07-17]
- Factions - positions & permissions: leaders make custom positions with tiered permissions - Green (use/loan items), Orange (organized crimes, finances, forums, applications), Red (remove members, adjust balances, manage wars and upgrades), and Black (newsletters/announcements). [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Faction | 2026-07-17]
- Factions - respect & chaining: respect drives Hall of Fame ranking and comes from attacking/chaining, owning territory, and organized crimes. When a member is attacked, the faction loses 25% of the respect the attacker gained. Chaining = consecutive hits within 5-minute windows (first 10 within 5 min); upgrades raise the max chain up to 100,000, with respect scaling logarithmically and bonus hits giving flat respect. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Faction | 2026-07-17]
- Factions - war & armory: Ranked Wars are structured faction-vs-faction fights. Raids cause 100% respect loss on every hit and need 10+ members; a raid ends after 24h if one side leads or 72h of inactivity. If a faction's respect hits zero it's permanently destroyed. The armory holds up to 100,000 shared items (weapons, armor, medical, drugs, boosters, utilities, consumables, points) to give or loan to members. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Faction | 2026-07-17]
- Factions - starting one: it costs $1,000 x the number of factions currently in the game to start one (or you can buy an existing faction cheaper via the forums' faction buying/selling thread). A brand-new faction starts with 5,000 respect and a "shed" HQ capping it at 5 members, upgradable later. If a leader goes inactive for 30+ days, leadership auto-transfers to the Co-Leader, or to the longest-serving member who was online in the last 24 hours if there's no co-leader. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Faction | 2026-07-17]
- Factions - applications: you can have up to 10 pending faction applications at once, edit or withdraw them anytime, and they expire after 72 hours if not actioned. After a decline or withdrawal you can't reapply to that faction for 72 hours. Accepting one application auto-removes your others. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Faction | 2026-07-17]
- Factions - destruction: if a faction's respect hits 0 (usually from a Dirty Bomb or a raid) it's permanently destroyed into an irrecoverable "death state" - the profile (name, image, past leaders) is kept forever, but all members are removed and every bit of money, points, and non-loaned items goes to the leader. Loaned items return to whoever loaned them. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Faction | 2026-07-17]
- Factions - chaining detail: a chain is consecutive hits on players OUTSIDE your faction. The first 10 hits must land within 5 minutes to start it; after that every hit must come within 5 minutes or the chain drops into cooldown. Respect per hit scales up (logarithmically) as the chain grows, and 13 set "bonus hit" points give a big flat respect bonus. A broken chain cools down for 6 seconds per hit made (a 10-hit chain = 60s). Starting a raid forces your active chain into cooldown. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Faction | 2026-07-17]
- Factions - attacking respect bonuses: base respect scales with the target's level, and these bonuses stack - fair fight (similar stats) up to x3, retaliation (hitting someone who just beat a factionmate) x1.5, attacking while overseas x1.25, group attack x1.25 (shrinking per extra attacker), and war attacks x2. Leave and Hospitalize give equal respect; Mug gives slightly less. If the target is in a faction, that faction loses 25% of the respect you gained (100% during a raid). [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Faction | 2026-07-17]
- Factions - raiding: a purely destructive war - every hit causes 100% respect loss (vs the normal 25%) and adds to your raid score, but earns you no respect. You need 10+ members to declare a raid, can only run one at a time (but can be raided by many at once). After 24 hours the leading faction can "Cease" it; the loser can surrender (the winner must accept to end it) or cease if no hits happen for 72 hours. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Faction | 2026-07-17]
- Factions - armory upgrade costs (respect, one-time and irreversible): Weapon Armory 1,000, Armor 1,412, Temporary 2,173, Medical 3,067, Consumable 4,719, Drug 6,662, Booster 10,251, Utility 15,773, Point Storage 22,265, and the Laboratory (dirty bomb) 105,027 which needs 25 members. A faction's inventory is capped at 100,000 items total. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Faction | 2026-07-17]
- Factions - capacity & territory upgrades: member capacity climbs I-X (5 to 10, 15, 20... up to 100), each level gated by faction age (e.g. capacity 20 needs the faction 10 days old, capacity 100 needs 150 days). Territory capacity climbs I-XVII (hold 1 up to 17 territories), each gated by holding the previous number of territories for 3 days. Core-branch upgrades (armory, capacity, territory, chaining, lab) can never be undone. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Faction | 2026-07-17]
- Factions - special branches: you can run up to 6 of the 8 special trees (Toleration, Steadfast, Aggression, Excursion, Fortitude, Voracity, Criminality, Suppression) and swap loadouts instantly. Unlocking more branch slots needs a number of active upgrades: slot II at 20, III at 45, IV at 75, V at 110, VI at 150 (and once unlocked it stays unlocked). Each successive slot doubles the base upgrade cost (1,000 in slot 1 -> 2,000 in slot 2 -> 4,000...). A special can't be un-set until 72 hours after you set it. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Faction | 2026-07-17]
- Factions - permissions & deletion: positions have four permission tiers - Green (using/loaning items, refills, faction API access), Orange (organized crimes, giving items/money/points, forum & application management), Red (kick members, adjust balances, war & upgrade management), and Black (newsletters, announcements, description changes). New members are locked to 'Recruit' for their first 72 hours. Only the leader can delete a faction, and only if it's under 100,000 respect with the armory emptied of all money and items; factions delete on an hourly cycle. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Faction | 2026-07-17]
