- Education (overview): Torn University offers 12 degrees, each a chain of courses that end in a Bachelor's degree (which grants an honor bar). Courses take real time (1-7 weeks each) and cost money, and most give working-stat gains (manual labor / intelligence / endurance); the standout ones also grant a permanent gameplay perk. Degrees: Biology, Business, Combat Training, Computer Science, General Studies, Health & Fitness, History, Law, Mathematics, Psychology, Self Defense, and Sports Science. [source: Torn API (education) | 2026-07-17]
- Education - Biology: key perks are +1% damage to all weapons (Evolution), +10% then +10% more medical item effectiveness (Intermediate/Advanced Biochemistry), +10% damage to throat hits (Neurobiology), +3% critical hit chance (Anatomy), -0.5 to opponent stealthiness (Forensic Science), and the ability to draw/deliver blood (Intravenous Therapy). Bachelor of Biology unlocks equipping needles in your temporary slot. [source: Torn API (education) | 2026-07-17]
- Education - Combat Training: mainly weapon accuracy - each weapon course gives +1.00 accuracy with that weapon type (Machine Guns, Submachine Guns, Pistols, Rifles, Heavy Artillery, Shotguns). Bachelor of Military Arts and Science unlocks weapon experience/specialization for increased damage and accuracy on individual weapons. [source: Torn API (education) | 2026-07-17]
- Education - Computer Science: the crime/tech degree. Intro to Computing unlocks the Cracking crime and Simple viruses; other courses unlock Polymorphic/Tunneling, then Armored/Stealth/Firewalk viruses, reduce virus coding time (-20% and -10%), cut rig heat -25%, and unlock rig overclocking (up to 30%, then 50%). Several courses feed Scamming (email extraction via data breaches / scrapers). Bachelor of Computer Science lets you send mail and cash anonymously. [source: Torn API (education) | 2026-07-17]
- Education - Law: the jail/crime-support degree. Perks include easier jail escape and less nerve to escape, buying yourself/others out of jail while jailed, -5% property cost, busting skill bonuses (+5% then +10%), and cheaper bailing. Bachelor of Law is the big one: busting is 50% easier and bailing 50% cheaper. [source: Torn API (education) | 2026-07-17]
- Education - General Studies: Driving License unlocks driving crimes, General Mechanics gives +1.00 accuracy with temporary weapons, General Science +5% temporary weapon damage, Survival Skills +15% hunting. Bachelor of General Studies gives a permanent 10% working-stat bonus to every future education you complete - so many players rush it first. [source: Torn API (education) | 2026-07-17]
- Education - History: an archaeology/treasure degree. Perks: +10% damage with Japanese blade weapons (Ancient Japanese History), +2% all melee damage (Medieval History), and the ability to find collectibles - medieval coins, Asian sculptures & Companion pages, Egyptian Senet pieces & amulets. Bachelor of History unlocks the museum, where artifact sets are exchanged for points. [source: Torn API (education) | 2026-07-17]
- Education - Self Defense: martial arts giving passive defense and speed bonuses (Judo, Krav Maga, Jujitsu = defense; Tae Kwon Do, Muay Thai = speed). Kick Boxing unlocks the kick attack in battle. Bachelor of Self Defense gives a huge +100% damage when fighting with fists alone. [source: Torn API (education) | 2026-07-17]
- Education - Sports Science: the gym-gains degree. Each course adds +1% gym gains to a specific stat (strength/speed/defense/dexterity), Sports Medicine gives +10% needle effectiveness, Sports Administration unlocks the sports shop, Nutritional Science +2% passive speed & strength. Bachelor of Sports Science adds a further +1% to all gym gains. [source: Torn API (education) | 2026-07-17]
- Education - Mathematics & Psychology: Mathematics gives passive speed/defense and ammo conservation (Discrete Math +5%, Bachelor of Mathematics +20%). Psychology gives passive dexterity bonuses, +10% awareness (Attention and Awareness), Scamming aids, and Bachelor of Psychological Sciences grants +10% passive crime XP. [source: Torn API (education) | 2026-07-17]
- Education - Business & Health & Fitness: Business courses boost your COMPANY (productivity, employee/advertising effectiveness, perceived product value); Bachelor of Commerce unlocks new company size, storage, and staff-room upgrades. Health & Fitness gives passive strength/speed/dexterity; Bachelor of Health Sciences gives +25% speed when an opponent tries to escape you. [source: Torn API (education) | 2026-07-17]
