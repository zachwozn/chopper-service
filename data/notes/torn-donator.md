- Donator status (overview): donating unlocks a 150 energy bar (vs 100), 5 energy every 10 minutes, advanced search, the notebook, donator forums, and donator-day medals. A standard donator pack is $5 (20% off with crypto), gives 31 days of status and 60 points. Packs can be bought in bulk with tiered discounts. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Donator | 2026-07-17]
- Donator - subscriptions: monthly ($4.85/mo) gives 31 days status, 90 points, two special monthly items, and entry into daily subscriber point draws. Annual ($48.50/yr) gives 372 days status, 1,080 points up front, and all monthly perks. Very large lifetime donors (~$1,000+) unlock blue username customization. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Donator | 2026-07-17]
