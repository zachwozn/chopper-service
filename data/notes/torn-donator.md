- Donator status (overview): donating unlocks a 150 energy bar (vs 100), 5 energy every 10 minutes, advanced search, the notebook, donator forums, and donator-day medals. A standard donator pack is $5.00 USD (20% off with bitcoin/crypto), gives 31 days of status and 60 points. You donate at the Donator House in the City (Financial district). Standard packs appear in your items list and must be "used" to apply; subscribers instead get days/points added directly to their account. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Donator | 2026-07-18]
- Donator - standard packs & bulk: you can buy packs in quantities of 1, 2, 3, 5, 10, 15, 20, or 25, with discounts on bulk orders. Each pack = 31 days status + 60 points. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Donator | 2026-07-18]
- Donator - subscriptions: monthly is $4.85 USD/mo (31 days status + 90 points every 31 days); annual is $48.50 USD/yr (372 days status + 1,080 points delivered up front). Subscribers also get two "special items" on the 1st of each month (things like a Cosmetic Cache, Supply Packs, or Books - the highest chance of these unique items), plus random giveaways and holiday gifts. Any leftover donator-pack days carry over and resume when the subscription ends. You can cancel a subscription anytime without losing already-paid status. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Donator | 2026-07-18]
- Donator - subscriber draws: subscribers are entered into daily "Subscriber Draws" where five subscribers are picked at random and awarded points - 10,000 / 5,000 / 2,500 / 1,000 / 1,000 points. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Donator | 2026-07-18]
- Donator - large donations & blue name: after buying 3 x 25 donator packs, an option unlocks to bulk-buy packs with extra bonus points (web only, via Amazon/PayPal/Crypto - not Googleplay). Around ~$1,000 USD total donated unlocks a Blue Name option (toggleable when picking honor bars). Large purchases get up to a 21% discount via Amazon/PayPal, with a further 20% off for Crypto. The max is 9,999 packs at once (~$39,465.05 USD); any order of 25-9,999 packs gets the 21% discount. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Donator | 2026-07-18]
- Donator - payment & support: pay via PayPal, Amazon, or Crypto in a web browser, or via Google Play in the official Android app. Crypto is accepted for donator packs only, NOT recurring subscriptions. Refunds are typically granted within 30 days if the goods are still recoverable from the account - contact payments@torn.com for refunds or any donation issues (you can also mail bogie [148747]). [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Donator | 2026-07-18]
- Donator - historical & removed rewards: before 3 Sept 2024 the point values were lower - packs gave 50 points, monthly subs 75, annual subs 900 (now 60 / 90 / 1,080). Crime Enhancers were once part of the subscriber rewards but were removed from the drop table when Cosmetic Caches were introduced, and Cesium-137 was also removed from the sub reward drop table. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Donator | 2026-07-18]
