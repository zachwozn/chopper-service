- New Player Missions (overview): a guided tutorial series that teaches new players the basics, narrated by an in-game mentor. There are 10 sequential categories - Getting Started, Dummy Bashing, Exploring the City #1-3, Criminal Methods, Guns Ammo & Other Players, Effective Stat Building, Well on your way, and Post Missions - each with step-by-step tasks (train, buy items, attack NPCs, visit locations, commit crimes). [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/New_Player_Missions | 2026-07-17]
- New Player Missions - rewards: each mission hands out energy (usually 50-100), a bit of cash ($250-$1,000), and 2 mission credits, so completing the whole set is a solid early boost for a new player. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/New_Player_Missions | 2026-07-17]
