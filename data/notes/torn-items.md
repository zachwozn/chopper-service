- Items (overview): items fall into categories - Equipment (primary/secondary/melee weapons, temporary weapons, armor), Useful supplies (medical, drugs, energy drinks, alcohol, candy, stat enhancers, tools, materials, supply packs), General goods (clothing, jewelry, flowers, plushies, cars, artifacts, collectibles), and non-tradeable Books. Items have rarity tiers (Uncommon, Limited, Rare, Very Rare, Extremely Rare) based on circulation. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Items | 2026-07-17]
- Items - getting them: sources include the Item Market, Auction House, East Side city shops, player trades, city finds, the Dump, NPC loot drops, crimes, and events. Weapons/armor sort by quality (highest first). Rare collectables have had their sell values removed so you can't accidentally sell them, and weapons auto-equip when sent to inactive players if a slot is free. [source: https://wiki.torn.com/wiki/Items | 2026-07-17]
